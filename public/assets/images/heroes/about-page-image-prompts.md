## Image Specifications & Recommendations

### Technical Requirements:
- **Aspect Ratios:**
  - Hero/Featured Image: 16:9 or 21:9 (1920x1080 or 2560x1080)
  - Section Images: 4:3 or 16:9 (1200x900 or 1920x1080)
  - Thumbnail Images: 1:1 (800x800)

- **Style Guidelines:**
  - Professional, high-quality photography style
  - Warm, welcoming color palette
  - Diverse representation of African professionals
  - South African business context
  - Modern, contemporary settings
  - Trustworthy and established atmosphere

- **Color Palette:**
  - Warm earth tones
  - Professional blues and grays
  - Accent colors that complement the brand
  - Natural lighting preferred

### Usage Recommendations:

1. **Hero Image (How We Got Started)**: Use Prompt 1, 2, or 3 for the main featured image
2. **Section Background**: Use Prompt 4 or 6 for background images
3. **Supporting Images**: Use Prompts 5, 7, or 8 for additional visual elements
4. **Mission/Vision Sections**: Use Prompts 9-12 for related sections

### Alternative Styles:

If you prefer illustration or artistic styles, consider:
- **Illustration Style**: "Professional illustration of diverse African business professionals establishing a non-profit organization, modern flat design, warm color palette, South African business context"
- **Minimalist Style**: "Minimalist representation of business fellowship and legal recognition, clean lines, professional color scheme, abstract business elements"
