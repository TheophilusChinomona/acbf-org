# ACBF RSA Website - Current Features & Future Plans

## What Your Website Has Now

### Public Website Features
Your website currently includes:

- **Home Page** - Professional landing page introducing ACBF RSA to visitors
- **About Page** - Information about the organization, mission, and values
- **Blog Section** - Showcase articles, news, and thought leadership content
- **Portfolio/Members Directory** - Display member profiles and success stories
- **Contact Form** - Allow visitors to get in touch with the organization
- **Membership Signup Form** - Enable prospective members to apply for membership
- **Search Functionality** - Help visitors find content quickly
- **Mobile-Friendly Design** - Looks great on phones, tablets, and desktop computers
- **Fast & Responsive** - Quick loading times and smooth navigation

### Current Website Capabilities
- Display member profiles and business information
- Share blog posts and articles
- Collect contact inquiries
- Receive membership applications
- Provide information about the organization
- Showcase member success stories and portfolios

## Future Development Plans

We have an exciting roadmap to enhance your website with powerful new features, organized into progressive phases:

### Phase 1: Member Portal (Login & Personalized Access)

**Member Benefits:**
- Secure login for all members
- Personal dashboard showing membership status and activity
- Update your own profile and business information
- Access member-only content and resources
- Browse and connect with other members
- View and register for upcoming events
- Download member-only documents and resources
- Track membership renewal dates

**What This Means:**
Members will have their own private area where they can manage their profile, access exclusive content, and connect with fellow members.

---

### Phase 2: Administrative Dashboard

**Management Tools:**
- Centralized dashboard to manage all website content
- Create, edit, and publish blog posts without technical knowledge
- Manage member profiles and applications
- Review and respond to contact form submissions
- Approve or decline new membership applications
- Send emails to members
- View website statistics and member engagement
- Upload and organize photos and documents

**What This Means:**
You'll have complete control over your website content without needing a developer. Update anything, anytime, from anywhere.

---

### Phase 3: Event Management System

**Event Features:**
- Create and promote events (conferences, workshops, networking sessions)
- Online event registration for members
- Calendar view of all upcoming events
- Automatic email reminders for registered members
- Track attendance and manage event capacity
- Waitlist management for popular events

**What This Means:**
Streamline your event planning and boost attendance with integrated event management tools.

---

### Phase 4: Enhanced Communication

**Communication Tools:**
- Automated welcome emails for new members
- Email templates for common communications
- Bulk email capability to notify all members at once
- In-app notifications for important updates
- Newsletter management
- Email logs and delivery tracking

**What This Means:**
Keep members engaged with professional, timely communications that go out automatically or with just a few clicks.

---

### Phase 5: Advanced Features

**Premium Capabilities:**
- **Payment Processing** - Collect membership fees online securely
- **Automatic Renewals** - Send reminders and process membership renewals
- **Invoice Generation** - Automatic receipts and invoices
- **Advanced Search** - Powerful search across all content
- **Document Library** - Organized storage for member resources
- **Analytics Dashboard** - Understand member engagement and content performance
- **Member Activity Tracking** - See who's active and engaged

**What This Means:**
Professional-grade features that save time, increase efficiency, and provide insights into your organization's growth.

## Implementation Approach

### Our Methodology
1. **Phase-by-Phase Development** - We build in stages, ensuring each phase works perfectly before moving to the next
2. **Your Input Matters** - We'll check in with you at key milestones to ensure everything meets your needs
3. **Training & Support** - You'll receive guidance on how to use each new feature
4. **Security First** - All member data and communications are protected with industry-standard security
5. **Scalable Growth** - The system can grow with your organization as membership increases
